NDC 2013 was a great conference

24 Jul 2013

This was my first time at NDC this year but I can tell you - it's a great conference. 8 tracks, lot of brilliant speakers (just look at agenda), atmosphere was fantastic, organization was excellent (did you hear a term - Continuous Food?). Don't you believe?

For all of you that couldn't attend - regret and... watch videos - they're available online now! Go and grab them on vimeo.

Here's couple of my recommendations:

  • Want to see SignalR rewritten from scratch? Watch "Under the covers with ASP.NET SignalR" by David Fowler and Damian Edwards
  • Watching closely battle between different js frameworks? See "Cage Match - EmberJS vs. Angular" by Rob Conery and Tom Dale
  • Want to github more effectively? Watch githubber in action: "Effective GitHubbing: The GitHub Flow" by Paul Betts
  • Don't be so antisocial you geek! "Being an Anti-social Geek is harmful" by Hadi Hariri

Let me know in comments which sessions were worth watching - I'll watch them and possibly add them to the list. I bet there is lot more to recommend than what I've seen so far!

Happy watching! And remember - sharing (knowledge in this case) is caring :)
